How to Play

Welcome to the Battleship Game. You will face an opponent, either a friend or the computer. Follow these steps to play:

1. Ship Placement

  • Each player must place their ships on their board before the battle begins.
  • Select the orientation, horizontal or vertical and position of each ship carefully.
  • You cannot overlap ships or place them outside the board.

2. Taking Turns

  • Once ships are placed, players take turns guessing the location of the opponent's ships by clicking on cells on their board.
  • If you hit an opponent's ship, the cell will turn red.
  • If your attack misses, the cell will turn orange.
  • A successful hit grants you an additional turn. You continue attacking until you miss.

3. Winning the Game

  • The first player to destroy all of the opponent's ships wins the game.
